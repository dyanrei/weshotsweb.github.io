weSHOTS Upgrade Services

With computer hardware, an upgrade is a term that describes adding new hardware in a computer that improves its performance. For example, with a hardware upgrade, you could replace your hard drive with an SSD and get a huge boost in performance or upgrade the RAM, so the computer runs more smoothly.

At weSHOTS, we offers different hardware upgrade to boost the performance of your device. The team ensures the we have the best computer technicians for your need by increasing the performance and making the overall device run faster and more smoothly. Lastly, have a variety of options of hardware parts to choose from.

We upgrade:

  • RAM
  • Graphics card
  • Hard drive
  • Optical (CD/DVD) drive
  • Processor (CPU)
  • Input and/or Output parts
  • and many other upgrades
